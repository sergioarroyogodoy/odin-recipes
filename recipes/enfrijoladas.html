Enfrijoladas

Description

Enfrijoladas are a classic Mexican dish that offers a delightful twist on traditional enchiladas. These comforting corn tortillas are filled with a savory black bean puree and folded into a roll. They are then smothered in a flavorful, earthy bean sauce, topped with crumbled cheese, fresh crema, and garnished with onion, cilantro, and your favorite salsa. Enfrijoladas provide a rich, satisfying taste of Mexican comfort food that beautifully showcases the versatility of beans in Mexican cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Corn tortillas
  • Cooked black beans
  • Queso fresco
  • Cooking oil
  • Sour cream
  • Onions (chopped)
  • Avocado (sliced)
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Blend cooked black beans and chicken broth until pureed
  2. Heat the bean sauce
  3. Heat tortillas
  4. Assemble enfrijoladas by spreading beans on the inside and rolling the tortillas into a cylinder.
  5. Top with more bean paste, crumbled cheese, and onions
  6. Add crema to taste
